What is Freestyle?

This swimstroke is where a swimmer is horrizontal in the water, face down with arms alternating in a push to pocket, moving out of the water with a rainbow arm, and finishing into a 11# arm position. The feet are kicking, knees rubbing and little fast kicks with floppy ankles. Breathing is done by the swimmer twisting their head to the side, one goggle in the water while one arm remains in that 11# arm positon and the other is lifting into that rainbow. Their face turns to look down once their arm begins to recover down into the water

Important for Freestyle

  • Arm Pattern
  • Kick Pattern
  • Proper Breathe Timing

Arm Pattern

  1. 11# Arms
  2. Push to Pocket
  3. Make a Rainbow
  4. 11# Arms
  5. Repeat with other arm

Kick Pattern

  1. One leg lifts and then falls while to other begins to lift
  2. Ankles stay relaxed
  3. Knees have slight bend with effort coming from the hip
  4. 6 Kicks for 3 Arms

Proper Breathe Timing

  1. 11# Arms
  2. Bubble Arm remains straight
  3. Breathing Arm begins proper movement
  4. Head twists onto the Bubble Arm
  5. One goggle remains in the water while taking the breath
  6. Breathing Arm falls back into 11# Arms while face twists back lookingdown
